01 — HOW IT WORKS

Every task is the same loop. Only the specialists change.

1 · CONVENE

A Lead sandbox spawns. Its only job: decompose your intent and dispatch.

2 · DISPATCH

Specialists spawn with narrowly-scoped access — repo, database, or web. Nothing more.

3 · EXECUTE

Each agent works in its own Firecracker VM. Coordination only via signed mesh messages.

4 · SYNTHESIZE

The Lead collects contributions, resolves contradictions, fills gaps with round-2 agents.

5 · DELIVER

Push, PR, email, document, or screen recording — the artifact comes to you.

6 · PERSIST + DISSOLVE

Sandboxes die. With optional Engram memory on, next time half the work is already done.
